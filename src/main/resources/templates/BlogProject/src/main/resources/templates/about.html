Tim

Tim is an experience computer professional with decades of background in both software development and automation testing. He has worked in both legacy procedural languages, and more modern object-oriented ones, such as Java. He possesses a continual desire to add to his knowledge base, thus being considered a forever or lifelong student titles he takes as badges of honor working in the computer field.

Tim served in the USMC Reserves, the Army National Guard and the Army Reserves, finishing his military career as a active duty Lieutenant Colonel with the US Army. He has worked for various governmental agencies such as DOD and the US Treasury, as well as civilian companies in both the life and health insurance industries, prescription benefits management and mail-order delivery, and also programming for high end retail.

For his next move, Tim is positioning himself to make the jump into cloud computing and AI, two areas he feels certain will greatly influence the direction of the computer processing fields and in fact all of society for decades to come.
